The Best Movies According to Lance

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

This 1994 drama, based on a Stephen King novella,
is often considered one of the greatest films ever made.
It tells the story of a man wrongfully convicted of murder who spends decades in prison,
forming friendships and finding hope.

The Godfather

Francis Ford Coppola's 1972 crime epic is a classic
that explores the Corleone family's rise to power in the American mafia.
It's renowned for its complex characters, powerful performances, and iconic scenes.

Schindler's List

Steven Spielberg's 1993 historical drama is a harrowing account of Oskar Schindler,
a German businessman who saved the lives of hundreds of Jews during the Holocaust.
It's a powerful and emotionally charged film that has been praised for its realism and sensitivity..
